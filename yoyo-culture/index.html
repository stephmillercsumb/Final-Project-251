Yo-Yos and Pop Culture

Yo-yos was a huge part of the biggest pop culture moments during the 90s. Yo-yos became very popular and were seen everywhere. So many people became obsessed and would use them constantly. Two of the most popular yo-yos was made by the brand Yomega. They made the “fireball” and the “brain” yo-yos. These were some of the fastest yo-yos made in that time. Other types of yo-yos that were popular were the glow-in-the-dark and color changing yo-yos.
